About me

My name is Ariana. I have been listening to Smino for 6 years. His lyrical word play and vocal stylings have influenced me to write and record my own music under the name Aria Labaria. I hope to develop my musical, as well as coding skills with this page, and to gain Smino another fan. Enjoy!
